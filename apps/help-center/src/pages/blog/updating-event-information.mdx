# Updating Event Information

You can easily update your event details anytime to keep your guests informed about any changes.

## Step 1: Navigate to the Event Management Page
1. Go to the **My Events** page.  
2. Click the **Manage** button next to the event you want to update.  

## Step 2: Edit Event Details
1. Click on the **Edit Event** option.  
2. Update the necessary event details such as title, date, time, or location.  
3. Once done, click **Update Event** to save your changes.  

Your updated information will automatically reflect on the event page for your guests to see.
