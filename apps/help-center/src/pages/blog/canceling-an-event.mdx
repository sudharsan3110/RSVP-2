# Canceling an Event

Learn how to cancel your event safely and manage the process on RSVP.kim.

Sometimes, plans change — and that’s okay. This guide helps you cancel an event smoothly while ensuring your guests are informed.

## Step 1: Open the Event Page
1. Go to your **My Events** page.  
2. Find the event you want to cancel and click **Manage Event**.

---

## Step 2: Go to the More Tab
1. On the **Manage Event** page, click the **More** tab in the top menu.  
2. This tab contains additional event controls and options.

---

## Step 3: Cancel the Event
1. Click the **Cancel Event** button.  
2. Type the **event name** to confirm your action.  
3. Click **Cancel** to permanently cancel the event.

---

## Step 4: After Cancellation
1. The event will no longer be visible to guests on RSVP.kim.  
2. Guests who registered will receive an email notification.  
3. If the event had paid tickets, follow your refund process to reimburse guests.  
4. You can still view the canceled event in your dashboard history for record keeping.

---

That’s it! Your event is now successfully canceled.  
You can always create a new event anytime from your **My Events** page.
