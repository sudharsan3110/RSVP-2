# Event Registering Process

Organizing events and managing RSVPs has never been easier! Follow these simple steps to register for an event using our platform.

## Step 1: Sign In to Your Account

1. Go to the **http://rsvp.kim/** and sign in using your email id.

## Step 2: Browse Available Events

1. Once logged in, head to the **Discover**.
2. Explore the list of upcoming events.
3. Here you can also go through past events to get an idea of the types of events we host.

## Step 3: View Event Details

1. Click on the event you’re interested in.
2. Review the event details, including:
   - Date and time.
   - Location (physical or virtual).
   - Agenda or speakers, if applicable.

## Step 4: RSVP for the Event

1. On the event details page, click the **Get Ticket**  button.
2. If the event requires host approval for attendees, you may need to wait for confirmation.
3. Once event approval is received, you’ll be notified via email.
4. If Eevent does not require host approval, you can click on ** Show Tickets** button.

## Step 5: Confirmation

1. Once registered, when you click on ** Show tickets** button then you’ll be redirected to tickets page and can see your event ticket.
2. Use this ticket for entry to the event.
3. If the event type is physical, then you can click on ** Get Directions** button to get directions to the venue.
4. If the event type is virtual, you’ll find the meeting link in your ticket.
5. You can also cancel event by clicking on **Cancel Ticket** button.

## Optional: Manage Your Registrations

1. Go to the **My Events** section of your account.
2. View details of all events you’ve registered for.
