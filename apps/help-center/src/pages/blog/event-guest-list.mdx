# Event Guest List

As an event host or cohost, you can view and download the list of guests attending your event.  

## Step 1: Navigate to the Event Management Page  
1. Go to the **My Events** page.  
2. Click the **Manage** button next to your event.  
3. Youâ€™ll be redirected to the Event Management page.  

## Step 2: Access the Guest List  
1. Open the **Guests** tab on the Event Management page.  
2. You can view the complete guest list displayed in a table.  
3. To download the guest list, click **Download Guest List as Sheet**.  

![Event Guest List](/Guest-list.png)

## Tip  
Use the guest list to keep track of attendees, manage RSVPs, and communicate easily with your participants.
